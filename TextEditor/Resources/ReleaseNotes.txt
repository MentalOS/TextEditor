What you need to know.

Release 2.0.0.4

1) Font Styles
--------------

   At the moment the Toolbar Bold, Italic and Underline only work individually.  
   This means you can have Bold OR Italic OR Underline ut not any combination of them.
   The Font Dialog box works fine.


2) The Tools Menu
-----------------
   
   The Menu items Spelling, Find and Insert Link have not yet been implemented.
